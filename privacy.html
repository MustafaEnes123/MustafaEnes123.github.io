Data Collection & Privacy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This page explains how we handle data on enesy.online. Our goal is to be transparent about the information we collect and how it is used to improve your experience.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This service collects anonymous data, such as which pages are most popular, how long visitors stay on the site, and the general geographic location of our audience. This information helps us create more relevant and useful content. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) like your name or email address through this service.

LinkedIn and External Links

Our contact information is provided via a link to a professional networking platform (LinkedIn). When you click this link, you will be leaving enesy.online, and the privacy policy of that platform will apply. We are not responsible for the data practices of external sites.
